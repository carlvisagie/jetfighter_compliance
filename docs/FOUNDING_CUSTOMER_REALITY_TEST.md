# FOUNDING CUSTOMER REALITY TEST

**PATCH**: ACQ-QUAL-5  
**Date**: 2026-06-12  
**Purpose**: Validate whether MSPs/SaaS are genuinely better first customers than manufacturing

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Validation Result**: MSPs and Software/SaaS companies are **confirmed** as better first customers than manufacturing, but with an important caveat — the current product catalog (CMMC-focused) fits **all** DoD contractor populations equally. The differentiation is entirely in **buying behavior**, not product fit.

Manufacturing companies need CMMC just as much as MSPs. But MSPs will **find the website**, **make decisions quickly**, and **pay without procurement delays**. Manufacturing won't.
