CMMC Level 1 Readiness Assessment – Session Script

Internal readiness workflow for live assessment sessions.

OPENING (0–5 minutes) Hi [Name], thanks for making the time today. Can you hear me clearly? Great. So this is your CMMC Level 1 Readiness Assessment Session. The goal today is NOT to do a deep audit or grill you on technical details. This is a guided, structured intake designed to give you a clear, practical snapshot of where you stand against Level 1 expectations. I’ll ask a series of straightforward questions about your company, your systems, and how you currently handle security-related activities. You do NOT need perfect answers. “I’m not sure” or “we don’t have that” is perfectly acceptable. I’ll take notes as we go, and after the session I’ll generate a short readiness report showing your score, your gaps, and recommended actions. We have about 45 minutes. I’ll keep us on track. Does that sound good? --- WARMUP QUESTION Before we start, is there anything specific you're hoping to get clarity on today? (Acknowledge their answer briefly, then transition.) --- SECTION A — COMPANY & CONTEXT (5–10 minutes) To begin, I’d like to understand the basics of your organization. 1. What is the legal name of your company, and roughly how many employees do you have? 2. Do you work directly with the DoD, or mostly as a subcontractor to primes? 3. To your knowledge, do you handle: - Federal Contract Information (FCI)? - Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)? 4. Have any primes or partners brought up CMMC or NIST 800-171 yet? 5. How would you describe your current IT environment? (Mostly on-prem computers? Mostly cloud like Microsoft 365? A mix?) --- SECTION B — KEY PRACTICES SNAPSHOT (10–25 minutes) Account & Access Control - Do all staff have unique login accounts? - Do you have a joiner/leaver process? - Do you disable accounts when employees leave? - Do you use multi-factor authentication anywhere? (email, VPN, etc.) Devices & Updates - Roughly how many laptops/desktops are used? - Do they receive regular security updates? - Do you use antivirus or endpoint protection? Backups - Do you maintain regular backups for critical data? - Do you know how long recovery would take? Policies & Documentation - Do you have any written security policies? - Have staff acknowledged or signed them? Training - Have staff received any security awareness training recently? Vendors & Cloud - Which major third-party tools do you use? - Do you evaluate vendor security? --- SECTION C — GAPS, RISKS & CONSTRAINTS (25–35 minutes) Now I want to get your perspective. 1. What areas do YOU feel least confident about? 2. Are there any known issues you’ve been putting off? 3. Are primes/auditors applying pressure yet? 4. What timeline are you realistically working with? 5. What are your biggest constraints — time, budget, expertise, or something else? --- SECTION D — RECAP & NEXT STEPS (35–42 minutes) Thanks, that gives me a solid picture. Let me quickly recap what I heard: (Recap 4–6 key points in 30–60 seconds.) Here's what happens next: - I feed your answers into our internal checklist and scoring model. - I generate a written readiness report with: - Your score - A red/yellow/green map of key areas - Recommended next actions You’ll receive the report within 24–48 hours. If it makes sense to go deeper afterward, we can outline fixed-price implementation — but that’s optional. --- SECTION E — CLOSE (42–45 minutes) Before we wrap up: Is there anything else you think I should know? Great. Thank you again for your time today. Expect your readiness report in your inbox by [timeframe]. Talk soon.
